About Us

Silpakar Industry is rooted in the vibrant tradition of local Assamese artisans. We showcase and support heritage crafts including terracotta, bamboo, handwoven fabric, and antique woodworks.

Our Motive

To uplift rural craftsmanship, preserve cultural legacy, and make traditional art accessible globally through fair pricing and digital innovation.

Product Categories

  • Bamboo Art & Furniture
  • Wooden Sculptures
  • Handwoven Bags & Clothing
  • Interior Décor
