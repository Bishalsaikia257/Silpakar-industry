Explore the richness of Assam’s traditional craftsmanship. Our artisans bring life to wood, clay, and bamboo through intricate handmade designs that speak of culture and heritage.

About Us

Silpakar Industry is rooted in the heart of Assam, where generations of artisans have mastered the skills of crafting unique products from bamboo, wood, terracotta, and natural fibers. We celebrate these timeless talents by offering authentic handcrafted items, supporting rural communities, and sharing our art with the world.
